Which Option Is Right For You?

Choose Budget If:

  • Summer/occasional use only
  • Limited initial budget
  • Willing to replace battery every 2-3 years
  • Basic computing needs (no high-power GPU)

Choose Premium If:

  • Year-round reliable power
  • Gaming PC or power-hungry equipment
  • Want to minimize long-term costs
  • May expand system later

UK-Specific Installation Tips

  • Panel Orientation: South-facing at 30-40° angle maximizes winter sun exposure
  • Shading: Even partial shading dramatically reduces output - avoid trees/chimneys
  • Winter Care: Lithium batteries perform better than lead-acid in cold (but avoid charging below 0°C)
  • Safety: All outdoor wiring should be in UV-resistant conduit
